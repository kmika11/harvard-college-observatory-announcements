
HARVARD COLLEGE OBSERVATORY

ANNOUNCEMENT CARD 6

WILK COMETARY OBJECT. — A cablegram
received today from Professor E. Strömgren
announces the discovery by Wilk, at Cracow,
of a comet in the following position:

"Direct motion one degree in four minutes.”

A radiogram received directly from Cracow
confirms the above communication.

HARLOW SHAPLEY

September 3, 1926
