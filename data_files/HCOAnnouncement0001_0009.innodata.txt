
HARVARD COLLEGE OBSERVATORY

ANNOUNCEMENT CARD 9

COMAS SOLA'S COMET. — The following
positions of Comas Sola's comet have been received
by cablegram, dated November 10, and by
telegram, dated November 8, respectively.

The first observation is by Krumpholz, of
Vienna; the second by Green, of Amherst.

HARLOW SHAPLEY

November 10, 1926
